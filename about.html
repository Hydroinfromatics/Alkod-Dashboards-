Village Overview

  • 🏠 Area: 947.18 hectares
  • 👨‍👩‍👧‍👦 Population: 2,126
  • 🏘 Houses: 413

Key Feature: Alkoda Tank

A lifeline for Alkod's residents, the Alkoda Tank supports:

  • 🌾Agriculture
  • 🐄Livestock

Challenges Before Rejuvenation

  • 🚫 50% silt-filled capacity
  • 🌱 Weed-cluttered tank bed
  • 🔒 Blocked inlets and feeder channels
  • 🏗 Weak and silted bund

Rejuvenation Efforts

By ICICI Foundation (2022):

  • 🌊 Restored water storage capacity
  • 🛠 Reinforced bund (5.5m wide, 581.4m long)
  • 🌱 Cleared silt, weeds, and encroachments
  • 📈 Positive impact on sustainability
