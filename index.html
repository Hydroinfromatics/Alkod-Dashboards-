Alkoda Lake Rejuvenation Project

About ICICI Foundation

ICICI Foundation is dedicated to fostering sustainable environmental change and empowering communities across India. Through its inclusive approach, the Foundation addresses critical challenges like water scarcity and promotes long-term resilience.

Key Initiatives:

Surface Water Management:

Constructed ponds, lakes, and check dams to ensure year-round water availability and support agriculture.

Groundwater Management:

Implemented rainwater harvesting systems with storage tanks and recharge shafts to replenish groundwater levels.

Inclusive Development:

Focused on underprivileged communities, co-creating solutions to meet local needs and promote empowerment.

Nationwide Impact:

Active in 91 blocks across 56 districts in 22 states, addressing water scarcity and ensuring sustainable water resource management.

About ICCW

The International Centre for Clean Water (ICCW) an initiative of IIT Madras has been established in 2019 as, a non-profit Society. Over the past five years, ICCW has developed and implemented innovative solutions in real-time water monitoring, industrial effluent management, community water security, water body rejuvenation and its environmental impact assessment of water body rejuvenation hydroinformatics for the water-health-energy nexus, safe drinking water, and capacity building. To scientifically measure the impact of these interventions, the ICICI Foundation partnered with the International Centre for Clean Water and Indian Institute of Technology Madras to develop a scalable and repeatable methodology for assessing the project's effects. This assessment would provide measurable data on how their interventions had improved groundwater levels, ensuring their actions were making a lasting, sustainable difference for the communities they served.
